Gingered Pear and Raspberry Pandowdy

Description:

This recipe is a standard pandowdy with a little added pizzazz from adding candied ginger to the biscuit dough.

The result is warm and a somewhat spicy comfort food staple!

Ingredients:

  • 1 tbsp unsalted butter, at room temp, for pan
  • 1/2 cup (3.5 ounces) granulated sugar
  • 2 tbsp plus 1 tsp cornstarch
  • Pinch of fine sea salt
  • 4 large pears, peeled, cored and sliced (2 lbs prepped)
  • 1 tbsp freshly squeezed lemon juice
  • 1 dry pint (2 cups or 9 ounces) raspberries, fresh or frozen
  • 1 tbsp cold unsalted butter, cut into small pieces
